Strawberry Rhubarb Pie



Build Her a Pie

A mouth-watering description of this absolutely delicious pie!

If you don't like Strawberry Rhubarb Pie, then life has failed you.

Ingredients

  • strawberries
  • rhubarb
  • pie crust

Instructions

  1. prepare strawberries
  2. prepare rhubarb
  3. mix strawberries and rhubarb
  4. add mix to pie crust
  5. bake at 375 degrees F for 20 minutes
